# Browser And UX Evaluation

## Pass Criteria

- The changed workflow is reachable and completes.
- Loading, empty, error, permission, and success states are coherent.
- Keyboard navigation and focus behavior work.
- Desktop and mobile layouts have no incoherent overlap, clipping, or overflow.
- Required requests succeed and the browser console has no new errors.
- Accessible names and semantic controls are present.

## Fail Criteria

- The workflow cannot complete.
- A role can perform an unauthorized action.
- State is lost or duplicated unexpectedly.
- Controls are inaccessible or hidden by layout defects.
- Console errors, hydration failures, or required network failures occur.
